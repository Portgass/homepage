Google maps location marker

Marks multiple locations on map at once. Very useful when you have excel tables with column containing locations.

When searching, first few locations are located almost instantly. The next ones take longer time. Google doesn't let people request too much information at once for free.

Can find center point for found locations.

Before initiating new search click the reload button.
